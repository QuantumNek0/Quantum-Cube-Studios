The Arcane Enchanter

The centerpiece of this add-on is the Arcane Enchanter, a powerful new block that allows you to harness the magic of enchantments like never before. By using vanilla diamonds as fuel, the Arcane Enchanter empowers you to attune books to specific enchantments, making it easier to obtain the enchantments you need without relying solely on chance.

New Gemstones

Expand your mining expeditions with four new precious gemstones:

  • Alexandrite
  • Sapphire
  • Ruby
  • Yellow Diamond

These gemstones generate naturally throughout the Overworld, blending seamlessly with vanilla ores, ensuring a balanced and immersive experience.

Attuned Books

Unlock enchantments with precision by combining a book with a unique set of three gemstones. Each combination results in an Attuned Book, ready to be used in the Arcane Enchanter to grant you the desired enchantment. Say goodbye to randomness and take control of your enchanting process!

Seamless Vanilla Integration

Designed with Minecraft's core mechanics in mind, Arcane Enchanter fits perfectly into your world without feeling out of place. The natural gem generation and intuitive crafting mechanics ensure that it enhances your adventure without disrupting the classic Minecraft experience.
