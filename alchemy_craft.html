Alchemy Craft (Add-On)

Ever wanted to dive deeper into the mystical world of alchemy in Minecraft? Well, say hello to Alchemy Craft—the ultimate expansion for all you potion lovers and magical farmers out there!.

Showcase

Brew Bigger, Better Potions

We’ve totally reworked the vanilla brewing system with tons of new recipes. Now your favorite potions are stronger and last longer (up to 2 hours!). Plus, we’ve added potions you never thought you could brew—like a Haste potion! Get ready to brew like never before.

Grow Your Own Ingredients

Forget hunting mobs for alchemy ingredients. With our custom block, the Infuser, you can now create magical crops! Infuse flower seeds with alchemy ingredients and grow your own supply in your garden. And they’re not just useful—they’re gorgeous. These colorful, custom flowers will make your world look better than ever!.

Endgame Alchemy Magic

Your journey doesn’t stop at potions! Work your way towards crafting the legendary Philosopher’s Stone, allowing you to transmute materials (yes, turn iron into gold!). Plus, with the Stone, you can brew the legendary Elixir of Life! Immortality, anyone?.

Seamless Vanilla Integration

Everything in Alchemy Craft feels right at home in the Minecraft world. The transitions are smooth, the design fits perfectly, and it’s fully immersive for those who love that vanilla vibe.
